About ShopFlow

ShopFlow was created by Anna Batsikadze, a Georgian girl who truly enjoys online shopping. Anna wanted to make the experience easier, faster, and smarter for everyone. From a simple idea, ShopFlow turned into a full project where shopping meets smart technology.

From the very beginning, Anna was supported by Shorena — the person who believed in the idea from day one.

Why Choose ShopFlow

  • • Personalized product suggestions based on your style and budget
  • • Price comparison across multiple stores in seconds
  • • Easy access to current deals and discounts
  • • Fast and simple shopping experience
  • • Designed with users in mind – no clutter, no confusion
